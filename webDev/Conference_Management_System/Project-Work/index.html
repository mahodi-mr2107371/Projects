Online conferences, also known as virtual conferences or web conferences, are events that are held over the internet, where attendees can participate from anywhere in the world using a computer, tablet or smartphone. These conferences allow people to connect and collaborate without the need for physical travel, making them a cost-effective and convenient alternative to traditional in-person conferences. The benefits of online conferences include reduced travel and accommodation expenses, increased flexibility and accessibility, the ability to record and access conference sessions for later use, and the opportunity to reach a wider audience. Additionally, online conferences often offer interactive features such as live polls, chat rooms, and virtual networking, which enhance engagement and participation among attendees. Overall, online conferences have become a popular and effective means of communication and knowledge sharing in various fields, from business and education to healthcare and science.
